# Pitch Distribution Plan: The Big Leagues Update

This document defines the exact probabilities for every pitch at every stage of the game under the new **Leagues System**.

## Season Structure
*   **Season 1**: Score 0 - 199 (4 Pitchers)
*   **Season 2**: Score 200 - 399 (Same Pitchers, +1 Trick Pitch each, Higher Speed)
*   **Season 3+**: Continues infinitely.

---

## League 1: The Rookie (Score 0-49)
**Identity**: "The Heat". Standard pitches to engage the player.
**Goal**: Teach timing (Fastball) and basic movement (Curve).

| Score Range | Phase Name | Probabilities | Speed Multiplier (Approx) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **0 - 9** | Warmup | **50% Fastball**<br>**50% Curveball** | 0.6x |
| **10 - 19** | Power Intro | **60% Super Fastball**<br>20% Fastball<br>20% Curveball | 0.8x |
| **20 - 39** | Speed Trap | **50% Changeup** (Teach restraint)<br>30% Super Fastball<br>10% Fastball<br>10% Curveball | 1.0x |
| **40 - 49** | The Exam | **40% ZigZag** (New)<br>20% Super Fastball<br>20% Changeup<br>10% Fastball<br>10% Curveball | 1.2x |

---

## League 2: The Technician (Score 50-99)
**Identity**: "The Disruptor". Stops, splits, and breaks physics.
**Goal**: Teach reaction to mid-air changes.

| Score Range | Phase Name | Probabilities | Speed Multiplier |
|---|---|---|---|
| **50 - 64** | The Brake | **50% Stopball**<br>15% Super Fastball<br>15% ZigZag<br>10% Curveball<br>10% Changeup | 1.1x (Reset dip) |
| **65 - 79** | The Floor | **50% Splitter**<br>20% Stopball<br>10% ZigZag<br>10% Super Fastball<br>10% Mixed Standards | 1.3x |
| **80 - 99** | The Exam | **40% Ghostball** (New)<br>20% Splitter<br>20% Stopball<br>20% Mixed Standards | 1.5x |

---

## League 3: The Ace (Score 100-149)
**Identity**: "The Hybrid". Combinations of previous mechanics.
**Goal**: Teach recognizing compound threats (Curve + Split at once).

| Score Range | Phase Name | Probabilities | Speed Multiplier |
|---|---|---|---|
| **100 - 114** | Hybrid Intro | **50% Curve-Splitter**<br>20% Ghostball<br>10% Super Fastball<br>10% Splitter<br>10% Stopball | 1.4x (Dip) |
| **115 - 129** | Deadly Arc | **50% Super-Curve**<br>20% Curve-Splitter<br>30% Mixed Legacy (Technician Pool) | 1.6x |
| **130 - 149** | The Exam | **40% ZigZag-Changeup**<br>20% Super-Curve<br>20% Curve-Splitter<br>20% Mixed Legacy | 1.8x |

## Season 2 Loop (Score 200+)
At Score 200, the game loops back to **The Rookie**.
*   **Global Speed**: +15% Base Speed.
*   **The Rookie** gains: **Ghostball** added to his "Power Intro" pool (10%).
*   **The Technician** gains: **Stop-Curve** added to "The Brake" pool (10%).
*   **The Ace** gains: **Super-ZigZag** added to "Hybrid Intro" pool (10%).

This ensures that even though you are back to "basic" pitchers, they have evolved.
