# Pitch Ideas

## Existing Pitches
*   **Fastball** (White)
*   **Curveball** (Pink)
*   **Changeup** (Gold)
*   **Super Fastball** (Red)
*   **Stopball** (Purple)
*   **Ghostball** (Cyan)
*   **Stop-Curve** (Purple -> Pink)
*   **Ghost-Curve** (Cyan -> Pink)

## New Single-Color Pitch Ideas

### 1. The Splitter
*   **Color**: **Dark Blue**
*   **Mechanic**: Travels straight like a Fastball for 75% of the path, then drops vertically (y-position increases rapidly) right before the plate.
*   **Challenge**: Forces the player to swing low/late.

### 2. The Wobbleball
*   **Color**: **Lime Green**
*   **Mechanic**: The ball vibrates or "shakes" randomly in small x/y increments throughout its flight.
*   **Challenge**: Makes the exact hit box hard to predict; visual confusion.

### 3. The Boomerang
*   **Color**: **Orange**
*   **Mechanic**: Curves outward significantly (off-screen or close to it) and then curves sharply back inward to the strike zone.
*   **Challenge**: Intimidating trajectory; requires trusting it will come back.

### 4. The Knuckleball
*   **Color**: **Grey** or **Silver**
*   **Mechanic**: Moves in a completely random, non-linear path (drifts left, then right, then left again).
*   **Challenge**: Unpredictable movement pattern.

## New Two-Color (Hybrid) Pitch Ideas

### 1. Super-Ghost
*   **Colors**: **Red** (Super Fast) -> **Cyan** (Ghost)
*   **Windup**: Flashes Red, then Cyan.
*   **Mechanic**: Moves at Super Fastball speed but turns invisible for the middle section.
*   **Challenge**: Speed + Invisibility = Pure reaction/prediction test.

### 2. Stop-Fast
*   **Colors**: **Purple** (Stop) -> **Red** (Super Fast)
*   **Windup**: Flashes Purple, then Red.
*   **Mechanic**: Pauses mid-air (Stopball), but when it resumes, it launches at Super Fastball speed (instead of normal speed).
*   **Challenge**: Disrupts timing with the pause, then demands instant reaction.

### 3. Ghost-Stop
*   **Colors**: **Cyan** (Ghost) -> **Purple** (Stop)
*   **Windup**: Flashes Cyan, then Purple.
*   **Mechanic**: Turns invisible, then **reappears** in a frozen/stopped state, then continues.
*   **Challenge**: You lose track of it, then it suddenly appears stationary, forcing a mental reset.

### 4. Super-Curve
*   **Colors**: **Red** (Super Fast) -> **Pink** (Curve)
*   **Windup**: Flashes Red, then Pink.
*   **Mechanic**: A Curveball trajectory but moving at Super Fastball speeds.
*   **Challenge**: Less time to react to the horizontal movement.

### 5. Split-Stop
*   **Colors**: **Dark Blue** (Splitter) -> **Purple** (Stop)
*   **Windup**: Flashes Dark Blue, then Purple.
*   **Mechanic**: Drops suddenly (Splitter), pauses at the bottom of the drop, then continues.
*   **Challenge**: Vertical movement + Timing disruption.

### 6. Wobble-Ghost
*   **Colors**: **Lime Green** (Wobble) -> **Cyan** (Ghost)
*   **Windup**: Flashes Lime, then Cyan.
*   **Mechanic**: Wobbly movement that turns invisible. Maybe leaves a "trail" or "glitch" effect?
*   **Challenge**: Visual chaos.
